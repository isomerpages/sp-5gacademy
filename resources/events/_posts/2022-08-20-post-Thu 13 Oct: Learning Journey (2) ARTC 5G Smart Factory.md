<h1>Learning Journey #2</h1>
<h2>ARTC 5G Smart Factory</h2>
<p>Globally, 5G up-take is on track to become the fastest adopted mobile
generation in history. As a gamechanger, 5G offers high performance, low
latency, and security benefits of wireless over traditional fixed networks.
With 5G expansion, businesses can enjoy digitalisation adoption with more
mobility, flexibility, and security.</p>
<p>Join us at the ARTC 5G Smart Factory and discover how 5G can support advanced
manufacturing for:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Next Gen Hyper-Personalization Line</strong> with 5G enabled testbed
for trials in the Fast Moving Consumer Goods and Logistics industry</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Process Digital Twin for remote commissioning</strong> where 5G
is leveraged to power Digital Twin and intuitive tools with diverse real-time
monitoring capabilities</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Proliferation of Augmented Reality with 5G</strong> for guided
workflow and traceable setup for inspection station, and AR-guided training
for machine operation</p>
</li>
</ol>
